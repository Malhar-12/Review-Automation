# Derma Reputation Launch Blueprint

## Brand Direction

Working brand idea:

**SkinSignal**

Simple positioning:

`Google Reviews + WhatsApp Growth for Dermatology and Aesthetic Clinics`

Other usable names:

- GlowReply
- ClinicRepute
- DermaGrowth
- ReviewGlow
- SkinLoop

## Core Offer

We help dermatology and aesthetic clinics get more 5-star Google reviews, respond faster to every review, and convert more enquiries into booked consultations through WhatsApp.

What clients get:

- Google review monitoring
- AI-generated review replies
- WhatsApp review request flow after visits
- Private feedback collection for unhappy patients
- Monthly reputation performance report
- Optional WhatsApp enquiry and booking follow-up

## Ideal Customer Profile

Start with clinics that have:

- 50+ Google reviews already, or active Google Business Profile traffic
- 1 to 3 doctors
- regular cosmetic or skin-treatment enquiries
- active WhatsApp usage
- poor or inconsistent review response habits

Best target cities:

- Bangalore
- Hyderabad
- Delhi NCR
- Mumbai
- Pune

1. We set up your review and WhatsApp workflow.

5. New reviews appear in your dashboard with AI-generated reply suggestions.
6. Your team approves responses and tracks results.

## Sales Call Script

Use this structure:

1. Ask how they currently collect Google reviews.
2. Ask who replies to reviews today.
3. Ask how quickly WhatsApp enquiries are handled.
4. Ask how many enquiries are lost or delayed each week.
5. Show the workflow.
6. Offer a 14-day pilot or founding plan.

## Delivery Process

### Week 1 Client Onboarding

- collect clinic name, doctor name, logo, and contact info
- collect Google Business Profile link
- collect WhatsApp business number
- collect review-request script approval
- collect clinic hours and enquiry handling rules

### Weekly Delivery

- review new feedback
- generate or approve response drafts
- send review-request batches
- monitor private complaints
- send simple weekly update
